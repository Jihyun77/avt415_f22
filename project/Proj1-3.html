P. P1.3: User scenario mapping

Jane recently ran out of her dog treats, so she wants to buy new treats and toys for her dog.
She browses the online pet supply store for her needs. She compares various treats and toys on a pet supply site called JellyPet and puts them into the cart.
Finally, she decided to purchase and place her order for her dog.

Actor: Jane
Motivator: Ran out of her dog treats
Intention: She needs to buy new treats and toys for her dog.
Action: Jane browses the JellyPet, an online pet supply shop and compares various dog treats and toys, and puts them into the cart.
Resolution: She Purchases and place her order.
